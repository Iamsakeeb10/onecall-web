# ONECALL FIELD SERVICES LLC ‚Äî Pearl & Steel Blue Palette

**Professional, refined, and crisp-neutral color palette for your website.** Clean hierarchy, elevated surfaces, and steel-blue accents rooted in precision, trust, and expertise.

---

## Overview

The palette keeps a **pure white/cool pearl base**, layered with **cool stone grays**, **steel blue**, and **deep navy accents** ‚Äî evoking reliability, precision, and premium craftsmanship.

* **Primary Surfaces** (white & cool pearls) ‚Äî backgrounds, cards
* **Deep Navy** ‚Äî headings, dark elements, footer
* **Steel Blue** ‚Äî CTAs, links, highlights, primary brand accent
* **Warm Brass** ‚Äî secondary prestige accent (badges, stars, trust indicators)
* **Text Colors** ‚Äî hierarchy and clarity
* **Hero Colors** ‚Äî consistent inner page hero backgrounds
* **Utility Colors** ‚Äî errors, success, dividers

‚úÖ Design Principles:
- Cool pearl whites and stone grays create a crisp, authoritative feel ‚Äî distinct from warm/earthy tones
- Steel blue accent drives CTAs, hover states, and interactive focus ‚Äî conveying precision, technology, and professionalism
- Warm brass reserved for prestige trust elements (stars, verified badges, checkmarks) ‚Äî adding subtle warmth without orange
- Alternating cool pearl surface shades maintain comfortable visual rhythm without harsh contrast
- All text/background combinations meet WCAG AA/AAA accessibility standards
- Deep navy footer and hero tones feel commanding and trustworthy ‚Äî ideal for field services and home solutions
